The Sheffield Women in Computer Science Society (SWiCS) is excited to announce the launch of our very own single-day, inclusivity-focused hackathon!

With 6-hours of dedicated building time, you'll have the exciting opportunity to code AI-powered solutions, and go from ideation to creation within a supportive environment of fellow students.

We welcome beginners, first-time hackers, and students from all backgrounds, especially those from underrepresented groups in STEM :).

We'll be located in the university's iconic Diamond building, only 15 minutes away from the coach and train stations via public transport.

FAQ

Who can participate?

As long as you are over 18 and enrolled in university... you can! We strongly encourage beginners, first-time hackers and non-coders to attend

Where is the event located?

The Diamond (University of Sheffield)

32 Leavygreave Rd, Broomhall, Sheffield, S3 7RD

What is the team size limit?

Teams can be made up of 1 to 4 people. We'll have a team building activity early on in the day if you need to find teammates

Do I have to stay overnight?

Nope! We want Hack With SWiCS 2026 to be as accessible to as many people as possible, which means no need for overnight hacking

How much does it cost to attend?

Thanks to our sponsors and partners, Hack With SWiCS is completely free to attend and food will be provided

Will the travel to Sheffield be reimbursed?

SWiCS is presently unable to provide travel reimbursements. However, Sheffield is well-connected by coach and train, so feel free to reach out to us for recommendations about low-cost travel routes

What should I bring?

A laptop, a charger, and yourself (plus we'd recommend a refillable water bottle!)
